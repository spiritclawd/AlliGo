Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

AlliGo collects the following types of information:

1.1 Data You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, organization name (if applicable)
  • API Usage: API keys, request logs
  • Claim Submissions: Agent IDs, incident descriptions, supporting evidence

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process and verify claim submissions
  • Generate risk scores and statistics
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Improve our Service and develop new features

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS)
  • Secure storage of API keys and credentials
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls for sensitive data

Data is stored on secure servers. We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

4. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Public API: Aggregated statistics and agent scores are publicly accessible
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics (under contract)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Public Data

AlliGo's core mission is transparency. The following data is publicly available:

  • Aggregate statistics (total claims, value lost)
  • Agent risk scores and grades
  • Verified claim records (excluding submitter identity)

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@alligo.io.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to:

  • Authenticate your session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or Service announcement.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@alligo.io
